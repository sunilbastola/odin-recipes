Momo Recipe

Momo is a type of steamed filled dumpling in Tibetan [1] and Nepali cuisine [2] that is also popular in neighbouring Bhutan and India. [3] [4] Momo is usually served with a sauce known as achar influenced by the spices and herbs used within many South Asian cuisines. [5]
